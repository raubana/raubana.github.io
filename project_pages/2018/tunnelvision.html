Details

This project is a singleplayer Garry's Mod horror/thriller gamemode and was an entry in the Gmod Store 2018 Summer Gamemode Competition. Unlike last time, this entry was completed alone (aside from the models used for the voltage tester and the cassette player.)


The project draws inspiration from a particular episode of Doctor Who called Don't Blink. It also feeds from my own fears as a programmer. In this gamemode you traverse the tunnels of a modified version of the roleplay map Bangclaw, solving logic puzzles while hidding from a killer statue.


Some unique features were used when making this gamemode, such as:

  • A custom Nextbot base, Robust Scripted NextBot.
  • Custom wire/logic systems and entities.
  • ...and more.
